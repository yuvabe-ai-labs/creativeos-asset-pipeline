## Context block (copy from here ↓)

**Brand:** Prakriti Sattva — premium Ayurvedic, handcrafted, cruelty-free, nature-derived,
practitioner-formulated skincare & haircare. Feeling: calm, intelligent, sensory, slow-luxury.
Philosophy: natural/holistic wellness rooted in Ayurveda & Yoga; "clean clinical actives"
from botanicals.

**Campaign tagline (use this for reels):** "Nature's Intelligence, Bottled."
(Website also uses "The Art of Conscious Radiance" and "Rooted in tradition. Modern in
approach." — see rationale doc; default to the campaign tagline for reel outros.)

**Audience:** Women 35+, USA. Concerns: dry / Vata skin, mature & ageing skin, sensitive
skin; hair fall, greying hair. Ayurveda knowledge ranges beginner → well-informed.

**Tone of voice:** Calm Ayurvedic practitioner, not a commercial narrator. Educational,
premium, claim-safe, warm. One idea per piece. Hierarchy from restraint, not hype.

**Products (6) & dosha:**
- Turmeric & Sea Buckthorn face moisturizer (Vata) — hydration; aloe, sea buckthorn, kokum butter, cultured ghee.
- Paduka Hand & Foot Balm (Vata) — herb-infused moisturizing bar; beeswax, plant butters.
- Nourishing Body Butter (Vata) — whipped triple-butter + sesame oil for very dry skin.
- Keshya Ayurvedic Hair Oil (Hair) — rosemary, amla, bhringaraj, curry leaf, hibiscus, fenugreek.
- Keshya Shampoo Powder (Hair) — shikakai, reetha, amla, hibiscus, aloe, licorice, neem.
- Rose Body Butter (Pitta) — rose, alkanet, licorice, almond, jojoba, shea, aloe; cooling.

**Visual benchmark:** Forest Essentials, Kama Ayurveda, Aesop, luxury spa editorials, slow
beauty films, tactile macro product photography. Warm natural light, shallow depth of field,
realistic product texture, sharp/readable label, final 4–5s still hold. Mature, elegant hands
when human elements appear.

**Editing rhythm:** Slow reveal → sensory close-up → one educational/emotional insight →
clean product still. NO fast cuts, bounce transitions, glitch effects, or aggressive zooms.
Typography small, spacious, premium; no bold display fonts or drop shadows.

**On-screen text system:** Intro short & tension-led · Body communicates one idea · Outro =
product name + Prakriti Sattva + soft CTA or tagline.

**COMPLIANCE (hard rules):**
- USE: helps, supports, nourishes, comforts, designed for, helps relieve.
- AVOID: cure, heal, treat, repair, prevent, medical claims, guaranteed results, before/after
  promises, allopathic terms.
- Disclaimer for product-benefit / testimonial reels: "Not evaluated by the FDA. For external
  use only. Patch test recommended."

**Hashtags:** 3–5, lower-case; always include `#prakritisattva`.

**Reel durations:** never below 20s. VISUAL 22–26s · TEXT 24–28s · VO 26–30s.
